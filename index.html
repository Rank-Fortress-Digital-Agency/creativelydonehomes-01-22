Top 3 Things To Splurge On For Your Bathroom Remodel

There are plenty of advice articles out there about how to stretch your renovation budget as far as possible. Yet, as remodeling contractor experts ourselves, we can appreciate the value of finding those details that are worth spending a little more on. This may be for your own personal enjoyment or maybe for the worth of the final project down the road. Either way, you should be aware of some of the most binge-worthy features you may want to implement into your own bathroom remodel in your Long Island home. Keep reading to learn more!

Read Post

1. Cabinets

Cabinets are not just for your kitchen– you need quality storage for your bathroom, as well. One reason this is so important is that you need quality storage and as much of it as possible. Towels, toiletries, your hairdryer– these are all items you need on hand but not in a visible location. Plus, good cabinets will last for years to come. In fact, invest in quality cabinetry so that during your next remodeling job, you need only refinish the existing cabinets and save yourself a great deal of time and money.

2. Countertops

Here is what matters the most when it comes to your countertops: the goal is high-quality and low-maintenance. Whatever the cost may be, you will be grateful for this. Too many homeowners try to save money with inferior counters, and that shows within a year of installment. Water spots, lack of shine, and other flaws tend to show up pretty quickly when you pay less for your countertops. On the other hand, pay too much for the best material there is, which also requires a great deal of upkeep, and you won't be any better off. Find the perfect middle-ground for your countertops and you'll have the high-quality, long-lasting surface you want without having to break your back maintaining it.

3. Plumbing Fixtures

Something that may surprise you is how much the right fixtures can do for your bathroom. For one thing, quality fixtures mean no leaks, repairs, or rusty spots, at least not any time soon. However, superior fixtures also help enhance the overall visual aesthetics. These seemingly little details can help so much with creating a cohesive appearance. Even the toilet should be one of those items that you choose only the best. For starters, this will help prevent issues like cracks or leaks from developing early on. This is especially important in high-traffic bathroom areas where a frequently used commode can begin to wobble. Choose your toilet for comfort, eco-friendliness, and a quality that will provide you with years of peak performance.
